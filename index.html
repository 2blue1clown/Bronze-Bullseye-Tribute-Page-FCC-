Pempheris Analis

(Formerly known as the Bronze Bullseye)

A story of identity theft

In 2015 when a group of inconsiderates formed an Ultimate Frisbee team. They chose a name, Bronze Bullseye, and the pempheris was overjoyed to finally be recognised. Pemphy had always thought it was special. It knows it is an agile fish, escaping the claws of hostile preditors many times. It was finally this fishes time in the sun.

Pemphy caught wind that the team was designing a logo. Surely it would include the fish's distinctive figure, black tipped fins and general preyish charm. The reality could not have been further from the truth.

Pemphy was devastated. These people did not understand his beauty even slightly. To achieve retribution he cursed the team to lose universe point until their attitude changed.

Quick Facts

  • Nocturnal
  • Recently discarded it's more well known name - Bronze Bullseye
  • Can grow up to 17cm in length
  • Lives along the coast of WA
  • Has body dismorphia due to serial identity theft
